3D Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play

Fireboy: Move with WASD. Safe in Lava. Dies in Water.

Watergirl: Move with Arrows. Safe in Water. Dies in Lava.

Both die in Green Acid.

Co-op: Stand on buttons to open doors or move platforms for each other.

Goal: Both characters must reach their matching doors simultaneously.
